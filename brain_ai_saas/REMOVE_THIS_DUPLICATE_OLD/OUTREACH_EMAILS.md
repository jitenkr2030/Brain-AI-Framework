## ðŸ“§ Email Best Practices

### **Timing**
- **Best days**: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Best times**: 9-11 AM or 2-4 PM (recipient's time zone)
- **Avoid**: Monday mornings, Friday afternoons, holidays

### **Subject Lines**
- Keep under 50 characters
- Include company name when possible
- Mention specific benefit or pain point
- Avoid spam trigger words

### **Email Body**
- **Length**: Keep under 150 words for cold emails
- **Personalization**: Include specific company details
- **Value proposition**: Clear benefit statement
- **Call to action**: Single, clear next step
- **Professional tone**: Confident but not pushy

### **Follow-up**
- **First follow-up**: 3-5 days after initial email
- **Second follow-up**: 7 days after first follow-up
- **Final follow-up**: 14 days after second follow-up
- **Move on**: After 3 attempts without response

### **Tracking**
- Use tracking links for Calendly/GitHub
- Monitor open rates if using email tools
- Track response rates and adjust templates
- Log all communications in CRM or spreadsheet
