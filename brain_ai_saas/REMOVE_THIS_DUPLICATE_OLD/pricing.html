Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Community and Startup plans? +
Community plan is perfect for exploration and development with full access to the core framework. Startup plan adds production-ready features like memory governance, audit logs, and priority support, making it suitable for commercial applications.
Can I switch plans later? +
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged the prorated difference. When downgrading, changes take effect at the next billing cycle.
Do you offer discounts for non-profits or educational institutions? +
Yes, we offer special pricing for registered non-profits and accredited educational institutions. Please contact us with proof of your status to receive a custom quote.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and bank transfers. For enterprise customers, we also accept purchase orders and can set up custom payment terms.
Is there a free trial for paid plans? +
Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for our Startup and Business plans. No credit card required to start your trial. You'll have full access to all features during the trial period.
